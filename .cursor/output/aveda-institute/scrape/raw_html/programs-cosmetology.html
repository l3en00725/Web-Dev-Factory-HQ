Cosmetology

Master the art of hair, skin, and nails in our comprehensive cosmetology program. Learn cutting-edge techniques while embracing Aveda's holistic approach to beauty.

  2. Programs
  3. Cosmetology
Our Cosmetology program is designed to transform your passion for beauty into a thriving career. You'll receive hands-on training in all aspects of cosmetology including hair cutting, coloring, styling, chemical services, skincare, makeup application, and nail care. As an Aveda Institute, we emphasize the connection between inner beauty and outer beauty, teaching you to care for your clients holistically. You'll work with professional-grade Aveda products made from pure flower and plant essences. Our experienced instructors provide personalized attention in a supportive learning environment. You'll gain real-world experience in our student salon, working with actual clients under expert supervision.

Career Outcomes

Upon completion, graduates are prepared for careers as: Hair Stylists in salons and spas Makeup Artists for special events and media Color Specialists Salon Owners and Managers Platform Artists and Educators

Curriculum Highlights

Core curriculum includes: Hair Cutting & Styling Techniques Hair Coloring & Chemical Services Skin Care & Facial Treatments Makeup Application Nail Care & Artistry Salon Business Management Aveda Holistic Philosophy
