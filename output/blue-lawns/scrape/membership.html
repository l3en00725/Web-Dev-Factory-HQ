What are the terms of your maintenance subscription packages?

On the first of the month your card will be charged for your fixed monthly cost including sales tax. If you chose to take the annual package for the savings, your card will be charged annually on the same day it was originally charged. Because our subscription program has different costs at different times of the year, you cannot just cancel your subscription without paying the remainder of what is due for the services provided. As an example, a bulk of your package's costs are completed in the spring. Your spring clean up, mulch application, sprinkler turn on , and lawn treatments. If you would like to cancel, you can. At this time we will prepare  a prorated amount based on the services performed. We like to be as transparent as possible, so feel free to ask any questions you have!

Why Am I Paying For My Landscaping For 12 Months If I Am Only Getting Service For Approximately 9 Months?

We are a 12 month business with 12 months of fixed overhead. This helps us streamline our back end so we can focus on our front end, which is you.
